# Privacy Policy

**Star Battle**
**Omara Technology Design**
**Last updated: May 2026**

---

## 1. Overview

Star Battle ("the App") is operated by Omara Technology Design ("we", "us", "our"). This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and your rights over it.

---

## 2. Data We Collect

### On First Launch (All Users)

When you open the App for the first time, we automatically create an anonymous session tied to a randomly generated identifier (UUID). No name, email, or device identifier is associated with this record. This session is used to sync your puzzle progress and streaks across app restarts.

**Data synced for anonymous sessions:**
- Puzzle completion status and progress
- Time spent on each puzzle
- Streak counts (daily, weekly, monthly)

### If You Create an Account

If you choose to create an account, we additionally collect:
- Email address and password, **or**
- A token from Apple or Google confirming your identity (we do not receive your Apple or Google password)

### If You Make a Purchase

In-app purchases are processed by Apple via the App Store. We use Adapty (adapty.io) to verify purchase receipts and manage entitlements. We receive a confirmation of your purchase (product ID and entitlement status) but do not receive your payment card details.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Data

| Data | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Anonymous UUID + gameplay data | Sync progress across app restarts; calculate streaks |
| Email address | Account authentication; account recovery |
| Purchase confirmation | Unlock purchased content; restore purchases |

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising.

## 5. Data Retention

- **Anonymous sessions:** Retained until you delete the app or use Delete Account in Settings.
- **Named accounts:** Retained until you use Delete Account in Settings, which permanently removes your account and all associated data from our servers.
- **Purchase records:** Adapty retains transaction records per their own policy. Apple retains App Store transaction records per Apple's policy.

---

## 6. Your Rights

### Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time: open the App → Settings → Delete Account. This removes your account and all synced gameplay data (progress, streaks, entitlements) from our servers.

### GDPR (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:
- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request erasure of your data (use Delete Account, or contact us)
- Object to or restrict processing
- Data portability

Legal basis for processing: **Legitimate interests** (providing the game service and syncing your progress) and **contract performance** (fulfilling purchases you have made).

### CCPA (California Users)

California residents may request to know what personal information we have collected, request deletion, and opt out of sale. We do not sell personal information.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below.

## 7. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.
