# Credits

## Themes

The color themes in this app are inspired by and closely emulate the following open-source projects. Each is used with attribution under its respective license.

- **Original** — Inspired by [GitHub Primer](https://primer.style), GitHub's open-source design system.
- **Seoul256** — Based on [seoul256.vim](https://github.com/junegunn/seoul256.vim) by junegunn. Licensed under MIT.
- **Primer** — Inspired by [GitHub Primer](https://primer.style), GitHub's design system. Licensed under MIT.
- **Rosé Pine** — Based on [Rosé Pine](https://rosepinetheme.com) by the Rosé Pine team. Licensed under MIT. [See their branding guidelines.](https://github.com/rose-pine/rose-pine-theme)
- **Gruvbox** — Based on [gruvbox](https://github.com/morhetz/gruvbox) by morhetz. Licensed under MIT.
- **Tokyo Night** — Based on [Tokyo Night](https://github.com/enkia/tokyo-night-vscode-theme) by enkia. Licensed under MIT.
