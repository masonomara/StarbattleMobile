# Delete Your Account

Star Battle is operated by O'Mara Technology & Design. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time, using either of the methods below.

## What gets deleted

When your account is deleted, we permanently remove the following from our servers:

- Your account record and email address (if you created an account)
- Puzzle progress and completion history
- Streak counts (daily, weekly, monthly) and streak archive
- Purchase entitlements stored on our servers

This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Access to previously purchased content cannot be restored after deletion.

**Note on store purchases:** Purchases are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). Those stores keep their own transaction records under their own policies, which are outside our control. Deleting your O'Mara Technology & Design account does not request a refund — refunds are handled by Apple or Google.

## Option 1 — Delete in the app

1. Open Star Battle.
2. Go to **Settings**.
3. Tap **Delete Account** and confirm.

Your account and associated data are removed immediately.

## Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you can't access the app, email us and we'll process the deletion for you:

[mason@omaratechnology.com](mailto:mason@omaratechnology.com?subject=Delete%20my%20account)

Please send your request from the email address associated with your account, and use the subject line **"Delete my account."** We use this to verify that the request comes from the account owner. We will complete the deletion within **30 days** and confirm by email when it's done.
