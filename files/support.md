## Common questions

### How do I play Star Battle?

Place stars on the grid so that every row, every column, and every outlined region contains exactly the required number of stars. No two stars may touch — not even diagonally. Tap once to mark a star, and tap again to add a dot for cells you want to rule out.

### Does the app cost anything?

Star Battle Free is free to download and play. A new puzzle is available every day. Optional one-time purchases unlock additional puzzle packs and the streak archive.

### How do I restore my purchases?

Open the App → Settings → Restore Purchases. Your purchases are tied to your account, so make sure you're signed in to the same account you used when you bought them.

### How do I delete my account and data?

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time:

- **In the App:** Settings → Delete Account.
- **By request:** email [mason@omaratechnology.com](mailto:mason@omaratechnology.com?subject=Delete%20my%20account) with the subject "Delete my account." We'll verify the request against your account email and complete the deletion within 30 days.

This permanently removes your account and synced gameplay data (progress, streaks, and entitlements) from our servers.
